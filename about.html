Our backs tell stories no books have the spine to carry.

Rupi Kaur

The story of our pregnancies, our child birth and our journey through postpartum is etched into our spines

We mean it when we say it's not about us, it's really ALL about you. We just happen to be one of you! espite my training as a Chiropractor i found myself in the middle of the pandemic traversing the world of pregnancy and postpartum related backpain, with a sleep despising, mum dependant two year old.

felt lost between different sales pages, all experts with conflicting advice. Whilst i struggled to self diagnose my pain. Even when i did settle on a plan, the fact my sleep deprived, touched out, human udder of a body couldn't action half of the advice, made me feel worthless.

It's the false promise of a simple solution to a complex problem. Our body, family dynamics and mental health are all completely unique and have a direct influence on whether or not we can action the sold exercises and healthy routines that are poised to support our recovery.

So where do we start? Who do we listen to? and how the heck do we actually action the advice?

Mothers Spines is a close-knit supportive community that has been created to bring all of the best experts into one hub. Experts ranging from back specialists to help use fully understand our pain, physiotherapists to help us regain our pelvic floor health, sleep consultants to help us tackle our night owl children, therapists to support our mental health and many, many more.
